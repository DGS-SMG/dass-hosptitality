About Us

Hospitality, done properly.

Dass Hospitality is a family-run business founded by Danny and Abbi. Between them they bring over forty years of combined experience, built on a single principle: every event deserves exceptional food, service and hospitality — whatever its size.

We shape our offering around what our clients need and want, with a genuinely can-do attitude at the heart of everything we do. Put simply, if you're happy, we're happy.

Our work spans crew and artist catering, immersive West End productions, corporate events, street food, weddings, private parties and much more. We love hospitality in all its forms, and believe no event is too big or too small to deserve our full attention.
