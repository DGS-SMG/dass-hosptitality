Our Story, In Motion

About Us

Hospitality, done properly.

Dass Hospitality is a family-run business founded by Danny and Abbi. Danny brings over 26 years in hospitality — from chef and front-of-house roles to menu and operational development for national brands across the UK and abroad — having opened sites within Pop Brixton, Market Halls and Box Park, served as Executive Chef at Shelter Hall Food Market in Brighton (six kitchens feeding up to 3,000 people a day), and cooked at Glastonbury and Selfridges. He was twice named highest-performing trader at Secret Cinema's Stranger Things and Dirty Dancing, and now leads Dass as sole resident caterer for artists and crew at Dreamland, Margate.

Together, Danny and Abbi bring over 50 years of combined hospitality leadership, spanning bars, restaurants, manufacturing, QSR, casual dining, food halls, pop-ups and start-ups across more than 350 locations nationwide.

Our ethos is simple: great-tasting food in a well-organised, upbeat and positive environment, with great customer service always at the forefront. Our work today spans crew and artist catering, immersive theatre productions, corporate events, street food, weddings, private parties and much more — we believe no event is too big or too small to deserve our full attention.
