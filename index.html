Our Story, In Motion

About Us

Hospitality, done properly.

DASS Hospitality is a family-run business founded by Danny and Abbi. Danny brings over 26 years in hospitality — from chef and front-of-house roles to menu and operational development for national brands across the UK and abroad — having opened sites within Pop Brixton, Market Halls and Box Park, served as Executive Chef at Shelter Hall Food Market in Brighton (six kitchens feeding up to 3,000 people a day), and cooked at Glastonbury and Selfridges. He was twice named highest-performing trader at Secret Cinema's Stranger Things and Dirty Dancing, and now leads DASS as sole resident caterer for artists and crew at Dreamland, Margate.

Together, Danny and Abbi bring over 50 years of combined hospitality leadership, spanning bars, restaurants, casual dining, food halls, festivals, pop-ups and start-ups across locations nationwide.

Our work today spans artist and crew catering, immersive theatre productions, corporate events, street food, weddings, private parties and much more — we believe no event is too big or too small to deserve our full attention.

What We Offer

Our Services

From backstage crew meals to full-scale immersive productions, we tailor every service to the occasion.

Artist & Crew Catering

Feeding the people who make the show happen.

From reliable, hearty crew meals to bespoke hospitality riders, we deliver stand-out food that keeps artists and crew fuelled. Every request catered, every dietary covered.

We've been the sole resident caterer at Dreamland, Margate for two years running — cooking for artists including HAIM, Nile Rodgers, Sugababes, Olly Murs, and Lionel Richie. In 2026, we're proud to be crew caterers for the Heritage Live series. Backstage catering that hits every note.

Consultancy

Full-service hospitality consultancy — from strategy, brand, and menu development through to operations, staffing, and live delivery.

We build food and drink concepts from the ground up: creative, costed, and built for consistency. Menus that guests love and kitchens can deliver, night after night — blending culinary creativity with commercial know-how.

Previous clients include Ivan Ramen, Big Has, Miradora, and Rivals Social.

Immersive Event Catering

We specialise in catering for immersive entertainment — food and drink that doesn't just accompany the event, it becomes part of the story. We create menus and service styles true to the narrative, built for high-volume delivery, and designed to perform commercially.

Current projects include The Greatest Showman: Come Alive, with a food and drink offer as bold and theatrical as the show itself. Previously, we delivered in-world catering for Secret Cinema — thousands of guests per show, without ever breaking the illusion. Newest to the line-up: Wake the Tiger at Westfield London, Shepherd's Bush launching Autumn 2026, where we're crafting an offer as otherworldly as the experience itself.

One-off spectacular, touring production, or long-running experience — we make every bite part of the show.

Private & Corporate Events

From intimate private dinners to large-scale corporate experiences, we design and deliver food and drink that turns an event into an occasion. Menus crafted around your story, your brand, or your celebration, executed flawlessly on the night.
